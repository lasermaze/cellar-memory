## Community Notes

### PCGamingWiki

> Replaces DOSBox and applies TombATI for improved support and graphics on modern systems. Also includes the Unfinished Business bonus chapters, improved cutscenes, missing music tracks, and applies the water color palette from the PlayStation version.
> Installation[4] Download Tomb Raider Automated Fix. Extract the executable anywhere on your PC and run it. Follow the installation instructions after pointing to your Tomb Raider 1 installation location. Decide on any of the included improvements, including higher quality cutscenes from the PlayStation or remastered cutscenes. Complete the installation. Edit tombati.ini and tombub.ini in the Patches folder for advanced settings. Launch dosbox.exe for a custom launcher that provides a choice between the main game and the bonus chapter, or launch tombati.exe to play the main game directly. launch tombub.exe to play the bonus chapter directly.
> Graphics feature State WSGF Notes Widescreen resolution Use Tomb Raider Automated Fix or TR1X. Multi-monitor Use Tomb Raider Automated Fix or TR1X. Ultra-widescreen Use Tomb Raider Automated Fix or TR1X. 4K Ultra HD Field of view (FOV) Use Tomb Raider Automated Fix or TR1X. Windowed Toggle with Alt+↵ Enter (when using DOSBox) or use Tomb Raider Automated Fix. Borderless fullscreen windowed Use Tomb Raider Automated Fix or TR1X. Anisotropic filtering (AF) Use Tomb Raider Automated Fix. Anti-aliasing (AA) Use Tomb Raider Automated Fix then force in the graphics card control panel.
