## Community Notes

### PCGamingWiki

> The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind is a singleplayer first-person and third-person ARPG and open world game in the The Elder Scrolls series.
> Morrowind (base game) - 1.2.0722.
> Download the Morrowind intro skip files.
> Apply the files into Video, replacing existing files if prompted.
> Configuration file(s) location
> Open the configuration settings in the registry.
> In the right hand window, change Screen Height and Screen Width to the desired resolution.
> Download and run Morrowind FPS Optimizer. (all versions)
> Choose Change Morrowind registry settings here.
> Change defaults to the resolution you want.
> Uncheck novice mode and set your field of view (FOV) on the misc/2 tab.
> In the right hand window, change Multisamples from 0 to the number of samples you wish to use (ie, 4 = 4x MSAA).
> In the right hand window, change Presentation Interval from 0 to 1.
> However, the issue may also be caused by the following:
> The system does not meet the minimum system requirements for Morrowind, or is using an unsupported video card.
> Outdated graphics card drivers. In this case, the user must update their drivers to the latest version, available from their respective manufacturer's website.
> Installing Construction Set disc
> Place Disc 1 in the CD-ROM drive
> Right-click on the CD-ROM drive and click on Explore
> Go to CSInstall, then double click on setup.
