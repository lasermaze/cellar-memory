## Community Notes

### PCGamingWiki

> Fallout is a singleplayer isometric and bird's-eye view RPG game in the Fallout series.
> A mod that patched the game to enhance, expand and improve upon the Fallout 1 experience.
> Patch compilation which contains several essential patches and bug fixes, allows for high and widescreen resolutions, and contains optional fan content. Appears to be outdated compared to more recent mods such as Fallout et tu, and has gone unmaintained for years, not receiving updates since 2016. Introduces gameplay changes, as opposed to just fixes, that change the vanilla experience - even when selecting the "Purist" option at installation.[citation needed] Contains well documented bugs that can make progress impossible, and have yet to be fixed.
